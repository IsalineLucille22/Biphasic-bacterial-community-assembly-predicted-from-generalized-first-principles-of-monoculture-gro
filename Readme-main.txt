README 

The community growth model has four main folders that correspond with different experimental parts.

SynCom-20-SMINT/
Has the scripts to run the simulations of the growth of the 20-member defined community of soil isolates grown in soil microcosms, and estimate the substrate-mediated species interaction parameters (SMINT)

SynCom-21-SMINT-PARINT/
Has the scripts to run the simulations of the growth of the 21-member defined community of soil isolates grown in soil microcosms, and estimate the SMINT-interaction parameters of Lysobacter plus the parasitic/predatory interactions (PARINT) in the SynCom-21

SynCom-21-liquid/
Has the scripts to run the simulations of the growth of the 21-member defined community of soil isolates grown in liquid culture

metatranscriptome-analysis/
Has the main cleaned derived data from the metatranscriptome experiment of the 21-member defined community grown in soil microcosms (SynCom-21 MT in Table 1 of the paper). Output used for main figures 6 and 7, and supplementary figures 4-7.
