The experimental datasets used for the training and validation of the SMINT-parameters by the simulated-annealing optimization (SAO) algorithm
1) abund_cfu_se.xlsx abundances for the SynCom21 community in liquid
2) MergedData.xlsx sheet 8 yields and sorted names
3) MergedData.xlsx sheet 9 maximum growth rates and lag times

Matlab files:

4) data_to_save_liquid_3.mat the output of the SAO for the SynCom21 in liquid saved in the .mat 
5) Zenodo_Liquid_SynCom21_21days_PaperStacked_abundances.mat output of the script Liquid_Plot_syncom_from_multi_simulation_data_CF_SynCom21.m containing stacked abundances
6)Zenodo_SynCom21MTz.mat output of the script Liquid_Plot_syncom_from_multi_simulation_data_CF_SynCom21.m containing day-7 abundances
