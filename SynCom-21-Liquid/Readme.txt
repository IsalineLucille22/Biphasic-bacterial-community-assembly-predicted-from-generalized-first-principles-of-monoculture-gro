README

This folder has three subfolders.

Data/
The experimental datasets used for the training and validation of the SMINT- and PARINT-parameters by the simulated-annealing optimization (SAO) algorithm under liquid conditions

The output of the growth simulations.

Figures/
Output figures produced by the scripts in Scripts/

Scripts/
The various MATLAB scripts and subroutines for the growth demography simulations and the SAO
